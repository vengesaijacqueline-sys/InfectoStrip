# InfectoStrip Impact Measurement Framework

## 1. Purpose of Impact Measurement

Impact measurement will allow InfectoStrip to evaluate whether the platform is achieving its intended goal of improving access to affordable, timely, and reliable diagnostic support.

The measurement framework focuses on healthcare access, diagnostic efficiency, user adoption, AI performance, and long-term health system impact.

---

# 2. Primary Impact Areas

## 2.1 Improving Diagnostic Access

### Challenge

Many patients face barriers to diagnostic testing due to:

- limited laboratory infrastructure;
- equipment breakdowns;
- high testing costs;
- delays in receiving results.

### Impact Goal

Increase access to point-of-care diagnostic support, particularly in underserved healthcare settings.

### Potential Indicators

- number of tests performed;
- number of healthcare facilities using InfectoStrip;
- percentage of users operating outside major urban centres;
- reduction in time required to obtain screening results.

---

# 2.2 Reducing Diagnostic Delays

### Challenge

Delayed diagnosis can result in delayed treatment, disease progression, and increased pressure on healthcare systems.

### Impact Goal

Enable faster access to preliminary diagnostic information.

### Potential Indicators

- average time from patient presentation to result availability;
- comparison between traditional diagnostic pathways and InfectoStrip workflow;
- healthcare worker feedback on decision-making speed.

---

# 2.3 Strengthening Frontline Healthcare Workers

### Challenge

Primary healthcare workers often operate with limited access to specialist support and laboratory expertise.

### Impact Goal

Support task shifting by providing accessible diagnostic interpretation assistance.

### Potential Indicators

- number of healthcare workers trained;
- user confidence before and after training;
- usability scores;
- reduction in interpretation variability.

---

# 2.4 Improving Diagnostic Consistency

### Challenge

Manual interpretation of colour-based tests can vary due to:

- lighting conditions;
- user experience;
- subjective judgement.

### Impact Goal

Provide more consistent interpretation through AI-assisted analysis.

### Potential Indicators

- AI model accuracy;
- sensitivity and specificity measurements;
- confidence score reliability;
- comparison with reference diagnostic methods.

---

# 3. Community-Level Impact

Future community-based implementation aims to improve access for populations that experience healthcare inequality.

Potential indicators:

- number of communities reached;
- number of individuals screened;
- geographic distribution of users;
- accessibility of services in rural areas.

---

# 4. Health System Impact

At scale, InfectoStrip aims to support healthcare systems by:

- reducing unnecessary delays;
- improving primary healthcare capacity;
- reducing pressure on central laboratories;
- enabling earlier identification of disease.

Potential indicators:

- reduction in unnecessary referrals;
- improved resource allocation;
- healthcare programme adoption;
- integration into public health initiatives.

---

# 5. AI Performance Monitoring

AI performance will be continuously monitored through:

- accuracy tracking;
- error analysis;
- bias evaluation;
- performance across different devices and environments.

Monitoring ensures that improvements in technology translate into safe and reliable healthcare support.

---

# 6. Sustainability Indicators

Long-term sustainability will be evaluated through:

- number of institutional partnerships;
- recurring diagnostic strip usage;
- operational costs;
- revenue generated;
- expansion into additional diagnostic products.

---

# 7. Long-Term Vision

The ultimate impact goal of InfectoStrip is to contribute to a healthcare system where access to reliable diagnostic information is not determined by income, location, or availability of specialist infrastructure.

Through affordable biotechnology and responsible artificial intelligence, InfectoStrip aims to strengthen healthcare delivery across Zimbabwe and beyond.
