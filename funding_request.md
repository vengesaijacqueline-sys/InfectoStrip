# InfectoStrip Funding Request

## 1. Purpose of Funding

InfectoStrip is seeking early-stage support to progress from concept development toward a validated prototype capable of undergoing technical and clinical evaluation.

The funding will support the development of an affordable AI-enabled diagnostic platform designed to improve access to point-of-care screening in resource-constrained healthcare environments.

---

# 2. Funding Priorities

## 1. Diagnostic Strip Development

Funding will support:

- refinement of the InfectoStrip UTI Plus prototype;
- development of biomarker detection zones;
- material testing;
- laboratory evaluation;
- quality improvement.

The objective is to develop a reliable paper-based diagnostic platform suitable for further validation.

---

## 2. Artificial Intelligence Development

Funding will support:

- development of the computer vision pipeline;
- creation and annotation of training datasets;
- machine learning model development;
- AI performance testing;
- model optimisation.

The goal is to create a reliable interpretation system capable of analysing diagnostic strip images under real-world conditions.

---

## 3. Mobile Application Development

Funding will support:

- development of the InfectoStrip mobile application;
- image capture workflow;
- user interface design;
- result presentation;
- integration with the AI interpretation system.

The application will be designed for frontline healthcare workers operating in low-resource settings.

---

## 4. Validation and Testing

Funding will support:

- laboratory testing;
- performance evaluation;
- comparison with reference diagnostic methods;
- prototype improvement;
- preparation for future clinical evaluation.

Validation is essential before any healthcare deployment.

---

## 5. Regulatory and Compliance Preparation

Funding will support:

- regulatory pathway assessment;
- ethical documentation;
- data protection planning;
- quality management preparation.

This ensures that InfectoStrip follows a responsible pathway toward healthcare implementation.

---

## 6. User Testing and Healthcare Engagement

Funding will support engagement with:

- healthcare workers;
- laboratory professionals;
- potential implementation partners.

Activities will include:

- usability testing;
- workflow assessment;
- feedback collection;
- training material development.

---

# 3. Expected Outcomes

With early-stage funding, InfectoStrip aims to achieve:

## Technical Outcomes

- functional mobile application prototype;
- improved diagnostic strip prototype;
- initial AI interpretation model;
- documented system architecture.

## Validation Outcomes

- preliminary performance testing;
- identified validation requirements;
- improved understanding of deployment conditions.

## Partnership Outcomes

- engagement with healthcare stakeholders;
- identification of pilot opportunities;
- stronger pathway toward implementation.

---

# 4. Long-Term Sustainability

Funding will support the transition from innovation stage toward a sustainable healthcare technology platform.

Long-term sustainability will be achieved through:

- diagnostic strip sales;
- institutional partnerships;
- healthcare programme adoption;
- expansion into additional InfectoStrip products.

The recurring nature of diagnostic consumables creates a pathway for continued operation beyond initial development funding.

---

# 5. Why This Investment Matters

InfectoStrip addresses a fundamental healthcare challenge: the inability of many patients to access timely and affordable diagnostic support.

By combining low-cost biochemical testing with responsible artificial intelligence, InfectoStrip aims to strengthen frontline healthcare systems and make diagnostic information more accessible where it is needed most.
